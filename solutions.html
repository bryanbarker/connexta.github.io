Distributed Data Framework (DDF)

DDF is an open source, extensible data integration and content management framework. DDF provides a content management system and internal metadata catalog for publishing and indexing metadata. This allows for complex queries including geospatial, temporal, contextual, faceted, and Boolean logic. The DDF architecture is built from the ground up around security and flexibility. DDF deployments in the Department of Defense (DoD), U.S. Intelligence Community (IC), and International Intelligence communities promote collaborative use of discovered data by analysts to produce enhanced Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR) products.

Alliance

Alliance builds on the DDF solution. Alliance extends the ISR capabilities of DDF and integrates out-of-the-box with many known ISR data formats and service interfaces. Extensions include international support including interoperability with the Coalition Shared Dataserver (CSD). Alliance is an open source project.

Connexta Enterprise Agreement

A Connexta Enterprise Agreement subscription provides the services needed to operate DDF and Alliance-based systems reliably and securely in your mission critical environment. More than a software package, an Enterprise Agreement unlocks direct access to engineering expertise, security updates, and support tools unavailable elsewhere. The four subscription tiers are designed to address individual enterprise requirements. The tiers differ with the number of sensors and users and integration requirements.

Includes a team(s) dedicated to the integration of DDF and Alliance in your organization for strategic and enterprise nodes.

On-Site Support

Includes engineers dispatched to your site to assist with installation, configuration, and administration.

Technical Support

Includes 24/7 availability, unlimited incidents, and phone, email, and online collaboration.

Continuous Delivery

Always get critical updates including patches, bug fixes, and security updates.
