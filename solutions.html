Distributed Data Framework (DDF)

DDF is an open source, extensible data integration and content management framework. DDF provides a content management system as well as an internal metadata catalog for publishing and indexing metadata, allowing for complex queries including geospatial, temporal, contextual, faceted, and boolean logic queries. The DDF architecture is built from the ground up around security and flexibility. DDF deployments in the DoD, US Intelligence Community (IC), and International Intelligence communities promotes collaborative use of discovered data by analysts to produce enhanced ISR products. DDF has been deployed for over 5 years in operational environments. The extensive use of DDF results in a stable baseline with continual improvements contributed from multiple organizations lowering the overall cost to implement this solution.

Alliance

Alliance, an open source project which build on DDF, extends DDF with ISR capabilities and international support including Coalition Shared Dataserver (CSD) interoperability.
